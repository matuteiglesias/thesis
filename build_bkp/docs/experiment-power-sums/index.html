Experiment 2: Power Sums

Experiment 2: Power Sums​

The aim of this experiment is to accompany the derivations in Section: Aggregating a Group of Agents (see Section: Aggregating a Group of Agents). The essence of the program is quite simple:

  • Generate vectors of length nqn_qnq​ with realized values DiD_iDi​ drawn from a theoretical distribution D(⋅)D(\cdot)D(⋅).
  • Compute the sum of values in the vector 10D(⋅)10^{D(\cdot)}10D(⋅).
  • Divide it by the sum of the zero levels (100=110^{\bm 0} = \bm {1}100=1), which is equal to nqn_qnq​.

The outcome is a time series of length TTT, and we store its moments, as well as the moments of its log levels.

import numpy as np
